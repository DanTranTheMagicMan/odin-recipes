Creamy Garlic Pasta

Description

This creamy garlic pasta will be the best pasta you will ever have. Whether you are a chef in a restaurant, making food for a potluck, or just cooking for yourself, this pasta works for every occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cups chicken broth, or more as need
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 pound spaghetti
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1 & 1/2 tablespoons dried parsley
